My Project Idea



Overview

For my project idea I have decided on making a simple but helpful mobile phone app for the placement of gas rams or gas struts which are one in the same thing just different names. It will be comprised of the strut lengths, strut gas pressure, door weights, and the recommended distance for attaching each end of the strut for each job required.

Motivation

My motivation for this app is to help fabricators like myself to be able to quickly and efficiently be able to work out the gas strut force and the gas strut positioning easily without the need to have to look up the required documents each time someone fits out the gas struts on which ever project they are working on at the time.

Description

My app that I would like to create will have all the formula embedded with in the program so the user only has to find which unknown they are looking for whether it be the gas strut force or the positioning of each end of the strut . It will have a graphical layout with a simple interface of a picture of the strut with all the information that is a variable will be blank and waiting for the user to input the desired amount or condition. The blank information will be the gas strut length as they come in many sizes, the gas strut force which is a requiem for opening the door or moving part on the project to desired height or angle. The door weight will also be needed to be inputted into the required blank spot as well as the angle or position that the door is required to open to. Once the user has inputted all the correct data my app will then display the correct placement and or the correct gas strut force needed to complete the said project or job they are working on at the time. All the formula needed for this app can be found online and is readily available to be inputted into my program.
